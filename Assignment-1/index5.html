1. Super 30

    • 2019
    • Main Actor: Hrithik Roshan
    • Description: "Super 30" is a biographical drama film based on the life of mathematician Anand Kumar and his educational program of the same name. The film showcases Anand's journey from his humble beginnings to his mission of coaching underprivileged students for the IIT entrance exams.

    2. Genius

    • 2018
    • Main Actor: Utkarsh Sharma
    • Description: "Genius" is an action thriller film revolving around Vasudev Shastri, a tech-savvy, academically gifted young man. He faces off against a terrorist plot and simultaneously deals with personal losses and love interests, ultimately aiming to save the country with his intelligence.

  3. Hanuman: The Legend

    • 2005
    • Main Actor: Utkarsh Sharma
    • Description: "Hanuman: The Legend" is an animated film that depicts the childhood and heroic exploits of the Hindu deity Hanuman. It is a tale of his strength, devotion, and loyalty to Lord Rama, capturing significant episodes from the Ramayana.

  4. Taqdeer

    • 2020
    • Main Actor: Naseeruddin Shah
    • Description: "Taqdeer" is a gripping thriller series that follows the story of a cold storage truck driver who finds a dead body in his truck. The series delves into themes of crime, investigation, and the unforeseen twists of fate that intertwine the lives of the characters involved.

  5. Avengers: Endgame

    • 2019
    • Main Actor: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, Mark Ruffalo, Jeremy Renner
    • Description: "Avengers: Endgame" is a superhero film and the culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. The Avengers assemble once more to undo the catastrophic damage caused by Thanos in "Avengers: Infinity War." The film explores themes of sacrifice, heroism, and the collective effort to restore balance to the universe.
